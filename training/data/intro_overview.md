# COMMODORE 64 APPLICATIONS GUIDE — Introduction

**Summary:** Describes broad capabilities of the Commodore 64 (word processing, budgets, games, music, animation) and points to community resources: local Commodore Users' Clubs, COMMODORE and POWER/PLAY magazines, and the Commodore Information Network on CompuServe for additional ideas and support.

## Overview
The Commodore 64 is presented as a versatile, general-purpose home computer capable of:
- Home and business budgeting and recordkeeping
- Word processing
- Arcade-style action games
- Music/sound generation
- Creating animated cartoons
The guide emphasizes that the C64 can perform all these tasks and more, and that ownership is valuable even if used for only one of the listed applications.

## Resources
For additional programs, ideas, and community support the introduction directs users to:
- Local Commodore Users' Clubs
- COMMODORE magazine
- POWER/PLAY magazine
- The Commodore Information Network (CompuServe)
