**Summary:** Connecting a VICMODEM to a Commodore 64 enables online communication with other computer owners worldwide. Joining the COMMODORE INFORMATION NETWORK on CompuServe provides Commodore product news/updates, financial information, shop‑at‑home services, and online multiplayer games.

## COMMUNICATION
Hooking a VICMODEM to a Commodore 64 provides basic modem-based networking capability so users can connect to remote systems and communicate with other computer owners internationally.

Joining the COMMODORE INFORMATION NETWORK on CompuServe gives access to:
- Latest news and updates on Commodore products
- Financial information and services
- Shop‑at‑home (remote purchasing) services
- Online games and multiplayer interaction with other users and user groups

This entry is an overview; detailed procedures for modem setup, dialing, terminal software, and printing or saving communications output are covered in related entries.
