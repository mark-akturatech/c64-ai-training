# ca65 Users Guide - The -v, --verbose option: increase assembler verbosity. Can be repeated for more verbose output; primarily useful for debugging purposes.

-v, --verbose
Increase the assembler verbosity. Usually only needed for debugging purposes. You may use this option more than one time for even more verbose output.
