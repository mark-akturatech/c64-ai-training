# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - Short JMP entries to reuse initialization routines: $FF81 JMP $FF5B (initialise VIC), $FF84 JMP $FDA3 (initialise SID/CIA/IRQ), $FF87 JMP $FD50 (RAM test). These are dispatch points used during reset/startup.

                                *** initialise SID, CIA and IRQ, unused
.,FF84 4C A3 FD JMP $FDA3       initialise SID, CIA and IRQ

                                *** RAM test and find RAM end
.,FF87 4C 50 FD JMP $FD50       RAM test and find RAM end
