# Contents - Technique to hide the raster color-change artifact: add a delay in the interrupt service routine before changing the background color to force the change into the screen border where the smear won't be visible.


By adding a delay in the interrupt service 
routine before changing the background color, you 
can force the color change area to be in the border 
where it won't show.
