# - C64 I/O Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - Register labels: $DC04 TIMALO (Timer A low), $DC05 TIMAHI (Timer A high), $DC06 TIMBLO (Timer B low), $DC07 TIMBHI (Timer B high).

$DC04        TIMALO       Timer A (low byte)

$DC05        TIMAHI       Timer A (high byte)

$DC06        TIMBLO       Timer B (low byte)

$DC07        TIMBHI       Timer B (high byte)
