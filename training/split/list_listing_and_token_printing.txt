# ********* - LIST: performs the LIST command by saving the range of lines to be printed in pointers ($5F-$60 and $14-$15) and printing the program lines, translating one-byte tokens back to ASCII text.

52652         $A69C          LIST
Perform LIST

This routine saves the range of lines to be printed in pointers at
95-96 ($5F- $60) and 20-21 ($14-$15), and then prints them out,
translating any tokens back to their ASCII equivalent.
