# NMOS 6510 - Unintended addressing mode: Absolute X Indexed (R-M-W). Lists undocumented opcodes (df, ff, 7f, 3f, 1f, 5f) mapped to DCP, ISC, RRA, RLA, SLO, SRE and shows equivalent legal mode ASL/DEC/INC/LSR/ROL/ROR behaviour in timing diagrams (3-byte, 7-cycle) and per-cycle bus activity. Includes note about dummy fetch and page crossing.
