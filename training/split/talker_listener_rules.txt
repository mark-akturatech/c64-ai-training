# Commodore IEC Serial Bus Protocol Reference - pagetable.com - Talker/Listener rules: only one talker may transmit at a time, multiple listeners can receive, controller orchestrates role changes by broadcasting commands, and all commands are heard by all devices on the bus.

Talker/Listener Rules:
  - Only ONE talker can transmit at a time
  - Multiple listeners can receive simultaneously
  - The controller orchestrates all role changes by broadcasting commands
  - All commands are heard by ALL devices on the bus
