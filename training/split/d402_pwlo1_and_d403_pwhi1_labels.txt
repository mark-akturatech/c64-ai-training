# - C64 I/O Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - Register labels: $D402 PWLO1 (Voice 1 pulse width low byte) and $D403 PWHI1 (Voice 1 pulse width high nybble).

$D402        PWLO1        Voice 1 Pulse Waveform Width (low byte)

$D403        PWHI1        Voice 1 Pulse Waveform Width (high nybble)
