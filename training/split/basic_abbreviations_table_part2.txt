# COMMODORE 64 - Continuation and completion of the Commodore 64 BASIC keyword abbreviation table (page continuation). Covers commands from LET, LIST, LOAD, LOG, MID$, NEW, NEXT, NOT, ON, OPEN, OR, PEEK, POKE, POS, PRINT, PRINT#, READ, REM, RESTORE, RETURN, END, EXP, FN, FOR, FRE, GET, GET#, GOSUB, GOTO, IF, INPUT, INPUT#, INT, LEFT$/RIGHT$/LEN/RND entries as they appear across the page break, and finishes with commands like RUN, SAVE, SGN, SIN, SPC(, SQR, STATUS, STEP, STOP, STR$, SYS, TAB(, TAN, THEN, TIME, TIME$, USR, VAL, VERIFY, WAIT. Also includes page headers/footers on the continuation page.
