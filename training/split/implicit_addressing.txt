# 6502 ADDRESSING MODES - Implicit addressing: instruction requires no operand because the target is implied by the opcode. Examples: CLC clears the carry flag; TAX transfers accumulator to X. Saves memory by storing only the opcode.

Implicit

TAX		Transfer the contents of the accumulator to index register X.
