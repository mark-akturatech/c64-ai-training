# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - CIA #1 serial and interrupt registers: $DC0C serial shift, $DC0D interrupt status (timer, TOD, serial, FLAG), and related control lines for serial bit shifting.

Serial Shift:

$DC0C   Serial Shift            Read/write bits on CNT pin edges

Interrupt Control:

$DC0D   Interrupt Status        Timer, TOD, serial, and FLAG interrupt status
