# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - Initializes filename/state variables: sets $A3,#$08, clears $A4,$A8,$9B,$A9 and returns. A compact initializer used before starting name/directory operations.


.,FB97 A9 08    LDA #$08
.,FB99 85 A3    STA $A3
.,FB9B A9 00    LDA #$00
.,FB9D 85 A4    STA $A4
.,FB9F 85 A8    STA $A8
.,FBA1 85 9B    STA $9B
.,FBA3 85 A9    STA $A9
.,FBA5 60       RTS
