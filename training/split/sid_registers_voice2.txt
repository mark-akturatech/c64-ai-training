# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - SID Voice 2 registers $D407-$D40D: $D407-$D408 frequency, $D409-$D40A pulse width, $D40B control, $D40C attack/decay, $D40D sustain/release (write-only).

Voice 2 ($D407-$D40D):

$D407-$D408  Voice 2 Freq       Frequency register (write-only)
$D409-$D40A  Voice 2 Pulse      Pulse width (write-only)
$D40B   Voice 2 Control         Waveform and modulation control (write-only)
$D40C   Voice 2 A/D             Attack and Decay length (write-only)
$D40D   Voice 2 S/R             Sustain volume and Release length (write-only)
