# - C64 RAM Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - $02A2-$02A4: indicators and save areas related to CIA #1 control during cassette I/O. Includes: $02A2 indicator of CIA #1 Control Register B activity during cassette I/O; $02A3 save area for CIA #1 Interrupt Control Register during cassette read; $02A4 save area for CIA #1 Control Register A during cassette read.

$02A2                Indicator of CIA #1 Control Register B Activity During Cassette I/O
                     
$02A3                Save Area for CIA #1 Interrupt Control Register During Cassette Read
                     
$02A4                Save Area for CIA #1 Control Register A During Cassette Read
