# - C64 I/O Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - Register labels for Voice 3 frequency control: $D40E FRELO3 (low byte) and $D40F FREHI3 (high byte).

$D40E        FRELO3       Voice 3 Frequency Control (low byte)

$D40F        FREHI3       Voice 3 Frequency Control (high byte)
