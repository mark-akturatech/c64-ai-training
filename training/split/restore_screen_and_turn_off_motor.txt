# A Minimal C64 Datasette Program Loader - Finalization: restore VIC-II screen by reading $D011, ORing in $10 and storing back to $D011; turn datasette motor off by ORing $01 with #$20 and storing it back to $01. Restores system state after loading completes.

Restore screen and turn off motor:

    lda $d011
    ora #$10
    sta $d011       ;screen on
    lda 1
    ora #$20        ;motor off
    sta 1
