# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - CLALL: Abort all files. Vectored from CLALL ($ffe7). Clears LDTND ($98) to zero (no open files) and returns. Simple STA to $98.

                                *** CLALL: ABORT ALL FILES
                                The KERNAL routine CLALL ($ffe7) is vectored here. The
                                number of open files are set to zero, and the next routine
                                is performed.
.,F32F A9 00    LDA #$00
.,F331 85 98    STA $98         clear LDTND, no open files
