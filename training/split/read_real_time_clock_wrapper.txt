# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - Wrapper at $FFDE that JMPs to $F6DD to read the real time clock into AXY (A most significant).

                                *** read the real time clock
                                this routine returns the time, in jiffies, in AXY. The accumulator contains the
                                most significant byte.
.,FFDE 4C DD F6 JMP $F6DD       read real time clock
