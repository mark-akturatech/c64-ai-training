# A Minimal C64 Datasette Program Loader - Turn on the datasette motor by manipulating CIA port $01: clear bit $20 (AND #$FF-$20) in $01 and store back to $01 to enable motor. This uses the system port at location $01.

Enable the datasette motor:

    lda $01
    and #$ff-$20    ;motor on
    sta $01
