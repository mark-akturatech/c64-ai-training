# 6502 Microprocessor - Implied addressing: no operand required, operation is implied by instruction (e.g., TAX $AA).

  4) Implied
  No operand addresses are required for this mode. They are implied by the
  instruction.
  eg.  TAX - (transfer accumulator contents to X-register)
       $AA
