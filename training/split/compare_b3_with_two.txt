# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - Tiny helper that loads $B3 into Y and compares it with #$02 (LDY $B3 / CPY #$02 / RTS). Used as a simple flag/selector check.

.,F7D2 A4 B3    LDY $B3
.,F7D4 C0 02    CPY #$02
.,F7D6 60       RTS
