# COMMODORE 64 - Detailed key-to-color mapping for quote-mode color control (CTRL+1..8 for primary colors and C=+1..+8 for alternate color palette), plus an example showing how to PRINT 'HELLO' in cyan then 'THERE' in white using embedded control characters.



      KEY           COLOR                APPEARS AS

   <CTRL+1>         Black
   <CTRL+2>         White
   <CTRL+3>         Red
   <CTRL+4>         Cyan
   <CTRL+5>         Purple
   <CTRL+6>         Green
   <CTRL+7>         Blue
   <CTRL+8>         Yellow
   <C=+1>           Orange
   <C=+2>           Brown
   <C=+3>           Light Red
   <C=+4>           Grey 1
   <C=+5>           Grey 2
   <C=+6>           Light Green
   <C=+7>           Light Blue
   <C=+8>           Grey 3



    If you wanted to PRINT the word HELLO in cyan and the word THERE
  in white, type:

    PRINT "<CTRL+4>HELLO <CTRL+2>THERE"
