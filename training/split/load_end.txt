# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - LOAD END: Final part of loader. Sets (X/Y) registers from end address ($AE/$AF), clears carry flag and returns (CLC/LDX/LDY/RTS).

                                *** LOAD END
                                This is the last part of the loader routine which sets the
                                (X/Y) register with the end address of the loaded program,
                                clears carry and exit.
.,F5A9 18       CLC
.,F5AA A6 AE    LDX $AE
.,F5AC A4 AF    LDY $AF
.,F5AE 60       RTS
