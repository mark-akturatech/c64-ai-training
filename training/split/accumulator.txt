# Architecture of 6502 Microprocessor - Accumulator (A), an 8-bit register used for arithmetic and logical operations (apart from increments and decrements). Data must be loaded into A to be manipulated by those instructions.


Accumulator		8 bits		A

Used for all arithmetic and logical operations (apart from increments and decrements).  Data must be loaded into the Acc before it can be manipulated.
