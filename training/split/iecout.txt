# Commodore 64 KERNAL Functions Reference - IECOUT ($FFA8). Writes a byte to the serial IEC bus (requires prior LISTEN/LSTNSA addressing). Input: A = byte to send. Real address: $EDDD.

$FFA8 - IECOUT
Writes byte to serial bus (requires prior LISTEN/LSTNSA). Input: A = byte. Real address: $EDDD.
