# Kick Assembler - Describes -asminfo option: java -jar KickAss.jar mysource.asm -asminfo all (output to asminfo.txt or custom via -asminfofile). Describes example asminfo output sections ([libraries], [directives], [files], [syntax], [errors]) and two categories of asmInfo: predefined (directives/libraries) and source-specific (syntax/errors). Examples of requesting 'allPredefined' or 'allPredefined|errors'.
