Registers

The 6502 only has 6 registers.  Five are 8 bits wide, one is 16 bits wide.

	A		Accumulator
			
	X		Index Register X
			
	Y		Index Register Y
			
PCH	PCL		Program Counter
			
	S		Stack Pointer
			
	P		Processor Status  (Flags)
