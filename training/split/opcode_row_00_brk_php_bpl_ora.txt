# NMOS 6510 - Opcode decode row for base 0x00 (lines include the header for columns +00..+1C and +01..+1D). Covers BRK, several NOP variants (zero page and absolute placeholders), PHP/PLP pair (PHP shown here), BPL relative, CLC, and the ORA family across addressing modes: (zp,x), zp, #imm, abs, (zp),y, zp,x, abs,y, abs,x. Includes the arrangement of official and undocumented NOPs used as fillers.
