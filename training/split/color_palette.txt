# The MOS 6567/6569 video controller (VIC-II) and its application in the Commodore 64 - VIC-II fixed 16-color palette encoding (4-bit values): 0 black, 1 white, 2 red, 3 cyan, 4 pink, 5 green, 6 blue, 7 yellow, 8 orange, 9 brown, 10 light red, 11 dark gray, 12 medium gray, 13 light green, 14 light blue, 15 light gray.

3.3. Color palette
------------------

The VIC has a hard-wired palette of 16 colors that are encoded with 4 bits:

  0 black
  1 white
  2 red
  3 cyan
  4 pink
  5 green
  6 blue
  7 yellow
  8 orange
  9 brown
 10 light red
 11 dark gray
 12 medium gray
 13 light green
 14 light blue
 15 light gray
