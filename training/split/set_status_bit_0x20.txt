# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - Simple helper that sets bit $20 in $01 (LDA $01 / ORA #$20 / STA $01 / RTS). Used to enable a control/status flag in the processor status word area.

.,FCCA A5 01    LDA $01
.,FCCC 09 20    ORA #$20
.,FCCE 85 01    STA $01
.,FCD0 60       RTS
