# - C64 I/O Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - CIA #2 Timer registers names: $DD04 TI2ALO Timer A low byte, $DD05 TI2AHI Timer A high byte, $DD06 TI2BLO Timer B low byte, $DD07 TI2BHI Timer B high byte.

$DD04        TI2ALO       Timer A (low byte)

$DD05        TI2AHI       Timer A (high byte)

$DD06        TI2BLO       Timer B (low byte)

$DD07        TI2BHI       Timer B (high byte)
