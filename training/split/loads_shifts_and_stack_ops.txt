# Kick Assembler - Entries for LDA, LSR and associated opcodes together with stack operations: PHA, PHP, PLA, PLP. Also includes ROL and ROR (rotate operations) and the interrupt/return instructions RTI and RTS as presented in the standard 6502 quick-reference table, with their opcode bytes by addressing mode.
