# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - CIA #2 serial shift and interrupt registers: $DD0C serial shift, $DD0D interrupt status (timer, TOD, serial, FLAG non-maskable interrupt status).

Serial Shift:

$DD0C   Serial Shift            Read/write bits on CNT pin edges

Interrupt Control:

$DD0D   Interrupt Status        Timer, TOD, serial, and FLAG non-maskable interrupt status
