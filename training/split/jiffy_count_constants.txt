                                *** jiffy counts
.:BF3A FF DF 0A 80              -2160000    10s hours
.:BF3E 00 03 4B C0               +216000        hours
.:BF42 FF FF 73 60                -36000    10s mins
.:BF46 00 00 0E 10                 +3600        mins
.:BF4A FF FF FD A8                  -600    10s secs
.:BF4E 00 00 00 3C                   +60        secs
