# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - Hardware vectors in ROM: NMI vector ($FFFA -> $FE43), RESET vector ($FFFC -> $FCE2), IRQ vector ($FFFE -> $FF48). Bytes stored at $FFFA-$FFFE.

                                *** hardware vectors
.:FFFA 43 FE                    NMI Vektor
.:FFFC E2 FC                    RESET Vektor
.:FFFE 48 FF                    IRQ Vektor
---

- "reset_hardware_startup" which expands on RESET vector points to hardware reset entry
