# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - CIA #2 timers $DD04-$DD07: Timer A ($DD04-$DD05) and Timer B ($DD06-$DD07), both 16-bit counters used for timing and interrupts for devices on the system bus.

Timers:

$DD04-$DD05  Timer A            16-bit timer counter (read/write)
$DD06-$DD07  Timer B            16-bit timer counter (read/write)
