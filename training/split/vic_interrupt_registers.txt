# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - VIC-II interrupt registers $D019-$D01A: $D019 IRQ status (raster, sprite collision, light pen), and $D01A IRQ control to enable raster/collision/light pen interrupts.

Interrupts:

$D019   IRQ Status              Raster, sprite collision, light pen status
$D01A   IRQ Control             Enable raster, collision, and light pen interrupts
