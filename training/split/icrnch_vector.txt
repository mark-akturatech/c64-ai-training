# - C64 RAM Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - $0304-$0305 ICRNCH: vector to the routine that 'crunches' ASCII keywords into BASIC tokens (CRUNCH routine at $A57C / 42364).

$0304-$0305  ICRNCH  Vector to the Routine That Crunches the ASCII Text of Keywords into
                     Tokens
                     
                     This vector points to the address of the CRUNCH routine at 42364
                     ($A57C).
