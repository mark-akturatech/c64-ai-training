# ca65 Users Guide - Documentation for the .MAX builtin function: returns the larger of two values. Syntax: .MAX (<value #1>, <value #2>) and an example showing reserving space for the larger of two data blocks (.res .max (.sizeof (foo), .sizeof (bar))).

10.18 .MAX
Builtin function. The result is the larger of two values.

The syntax is


        .MAX (<value #1>, <value #2>)
  
Example:


        ; Reserve space for the larger of two data blocks
        savearea:       .res .max (.sizeof (foo), .sizeof (bar))
  
See: .MIN
