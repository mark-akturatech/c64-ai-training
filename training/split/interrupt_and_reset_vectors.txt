# Architecture of 6502 Microprocessor - Reserved vector addresses at the top of memory: $FFFA/$FFFB hold the NMI handler address, $FFFC/$FFFD hold the power-on reset (reset vector) address, and $FFFE/$FFFF hold the BRK/IRQ interrupt handler address.

Other reserved locations:

$FFFA		Address of NMI handler
$FFFB		ditto
$FFFC		Address of power on reset location
$FFFD		ditto
$FFFE		BRK / interrupt request handler
$FFFF		ditto
