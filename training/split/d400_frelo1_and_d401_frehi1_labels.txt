# - C64 I/O Map (Mapping The Commodore 64) - Register labels: $D400 FRELO1 (Voice 1 frequency low byte) and $D401 FREHI1 (Voice 1 frequency high byte).

$D400        FRELO1       Voice 1 Frequency Control (low byte)

$D401        FREHI1       Voice 1 Frequency Control (high byte)
