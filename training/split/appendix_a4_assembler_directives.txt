# Kick Assembler - Appendix A.4 — Assembler Directives (Table A.7). Lists directives, examples, and descriptions for many assembler directives: '* = (origin)', .align, .assert, .asserterror, .break, .by (alias for .byte), .byte, .const, .cpu (e.g. .cpu _65c02), .define, .disk (create d64 image), .dw (alias for .dword), and .dword, along with their usage examples and brief explanations. Includes the trailing lines up to the chunk end.
