# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - Increments indirect address low/high bytes: INC $AC ; BNE (skip) ; INC $AD ; RTS. Small carry-propagating helper to advance an indirect pointer pair. Includes the following blank line (end of original chunk).

.,FCDB E6 AC    INC $AC
.,FCDD D0 02    BNE $FCE1
.,FCDF E6 AD    INC $AD
.,FCE1 60       RTS
