# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - SGN() entry: JSR to get FAC1 sign (returns A=$FF for negative, A=$01 for positive, A=$00 for zero). This wrapper simply calls the sign routine and returns the result in A.


                                *** perform SGN()
.,BC39 20 2B BC JSR $BC2B       get FAC1 sign, return A = $FF -ve, A = $01 +ve
