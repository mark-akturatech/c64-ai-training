# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - 8-ROM IDENTIFIER: Five-byte identifier string 'CBM80' stored at $FD10 used by autostart cartridges. Bytes: C3 C2 CD 38 30 (ASCII CBM80 in CBM encoding).

                                *** 8-ROM IDENTIFYER
                                The following 5 bytes contains the 8-ROM identifier,
                                reading "CBM80" with CBM ASCII. It is used with
                                autostart cartridges. See $fd02.
.:FD10 C3 C2 CD 38 30           CBM80
