# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - Reset vector entry: JSR $FC93 (restore everything for STOP) and branch always to restore/exit. Used as reset vector handler entry point.

                                *** reset vector
.,FCB8 20 93 FC JSR $FC93       restore everything for STOP
.,FCBB F0 97    BEQ $FC54       restore registers and exit interrupt, branch always
