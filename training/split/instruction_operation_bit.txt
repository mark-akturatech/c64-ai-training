# 6502 Microprocessor - BIT pseudocode: copy bit7 of src to Sign, bit6 to Overflow, set Zero based on A AND src.

/* BIT */
    SET_SIGN(src);
    SET_OVERFLOW(0x40 & src);	/* Copy bit 6 to OVERFLOW flag. */
    SET_ZERO(src & AC);
