# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - CIA #2 Time-of-Day clock $DD08-$DD0B: TOD tenths ($DD08), seconds ($DD09), minutes ($DD0A), hours ($DD0B) in BCD with AM/PM bit. Mirrors CIA#1 TOD functionality for additional hardware timing.

Time of Day:

$DD08   TOD Tenths              Tenth-seconds counter in BCD (0-9)
$DD09   TOD Seconds             Seconds counter in BCD (0-59)
$DD0A   TOD Minutes             Minutes counter in BCD (0-59)
$DD0B   TOD Hours               Hours counter in BCD with AM/PM bit
