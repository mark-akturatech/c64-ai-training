# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL ROM Disassembly (English, "CBM") - INCSAL (INCR): increments the two-byte SAL source address (low byte at $AC, high at $AD). Increments low, branches on no-wrap; on overflow increments high byte, then returns.

                                ;INCREMENT ADDRESS POINTER SAL
                                ;
.,FCDB E6 AC    INC $AC         INCSAL INC SAL
.,FCDD D0 02    BNE $FCE1       BNE    INCR
.,FCDF E6 AD    INC $AD         INC    SAH
.,FCE1 60       RTS             INCR   RTS
