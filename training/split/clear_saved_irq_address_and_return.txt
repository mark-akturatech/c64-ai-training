# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - Clear the saved IRQ address high byte ($02A0) and return (RTS). This is the cleanup step taken when a tape write operation finishes successfully.

                                *** clear saved IRQ address
.,F8DC A9 00    LDA #$00        clear A
.,F8DE 8D A0 02 STA $02A0       clear saved IRQ address high byte
.,F8E1 60       RTS
