# Commodore IEC Serial Bus Protocol Reference - pagetable.com - Primary device addressing: devices have Primary Addresses (PA) 0-30. Conventional assignments: 4-5 printers, 6-7 plotters, 8-11 disk drives/hard disks, 12-30 third-party devices.

Primary Addressing:
  Each device receives a unique primary address (PA) from 0-30.

  Conventional address assignments:
    4-5:   Printers
    6-7:   Plotters
    8-11:  Disk drives and hard disks
    12-30: Third-party devices
