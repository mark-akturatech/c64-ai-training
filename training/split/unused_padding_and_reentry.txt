# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - Unused byte(s) (0x03) followed by a jump to re-run VIC/screen initialisation ($FF5B). Acts as small padding/unusued area.

                                *** unused
.:FF80 03

                                *** initialise VIC and screen editor
.,FF81 4C 5B FF JMP $FF5B       initialise VIC and screen editor
