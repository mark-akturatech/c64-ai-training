# Commodore 64 KERNAL Functions Reference - IOINIT ($FF84). Initializes CIA chips and SID volume, configures memory, and establishes the interrupt timer. No input/output parameters. Uses registers A, X. Real address: $FDA3.

$FF84 - IOINIT
Initializes CIA chips and SID volume, configures memory, establishes interrupt timer. No input/output parameters. Uses A, X registers. Real address: $FDA3.
