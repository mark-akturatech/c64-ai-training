# Kick Assembler - Table A.4: Illegal 6502 mnemonics and their opcode bytes. Lists undocumented/illegal opcodes and mnemonics such as AHX/SHA, ALR/ASR, ANC/ANC2, ARR, AXS/SBX, ISC/INS/ISB, LAS/LAE/LDS and more with opcode hex values for the addressing modes.
