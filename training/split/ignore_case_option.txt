# ca65 Users Guide - The -i, --ignore-case option: makes the assembler case-insensitive for identifiers and labels. Overrides the default but can be overridden by the .CASE control command in source.

-i, --ignore-case
This option makes the assembler case insensitive on identifiers and labels. This option will override the default, but may itself be overridden by the .CASE control command.
