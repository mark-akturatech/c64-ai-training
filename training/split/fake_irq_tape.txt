# - Commented Commodore 64 KERNAL Disassembly (Magnus Nyman) - FAKE IRQ TAPE: Small helper that manipulates processor flags on the stack (PHP/PLA) and clears bit 4 of a stored status byte (AND #$EF) to fake/clear a tape IRQ condition. Used when simulating or handling tape IRQ state transitions.

.,FF43 08       PHP             store processor reg.
.,FF44 68       PLA             get reg
.,FF45 29 EF    AND #$EF        clear bit4
