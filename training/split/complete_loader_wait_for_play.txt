# A Minimal C64 Datasette Program Loader - Start of the complete loader implementation: wait for PLAY button before starting (polling bit $01 with BIT instruction while LDA #$10 prepares tape control). This is the initial entry point before disabling interrupts and preparing the screen/motor.

Complete Loader Implementation
------------------------------

Wait for PLAY button:

    lda #$10
    m1: bit $01
        bne m1
