# Commodore 64 Complete Memory Map - Source: sta.c64.org - CIA #1 timers $DC04-$DC07: Timer A ($DC04-$DC05) and Timer B ($DC06-$DC07), both 16-bit read/write counters used for interrupts and timing by the system.

Timers:

$DC04-$DC05  Timer A            16-bit timer counter (read/write)
$DC06-$DC07  Timer B            16-bit timer counter (read/write)
