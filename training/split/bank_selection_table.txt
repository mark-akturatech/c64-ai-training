# VIC-II Memory Banking and Address Configuration Guide - Bank selection mapping: table of CIA-2 $DD00 bit patterns (bits 0-1) to VIC bank address ranges and whether the ROM charset shadow is present.
- Bank 0: bit pattern xx11 -> $0000-$3FFF (ROM charset visible at $1000-$1FFF)
- Bank 1: xx10 -> $4000-$7FFF (no ROM charset shadow)
- Bank 2: xx01 -> $8000-$BFFF (ROM charset visible at $9000-$9FFF)
- Bank 3: xx00 -> $C000-$FFFF (no ROM charset shadow)

Bank Selection Table
--------------------

  Bank | Bit Pattern at $DD00 | Address Range        | ROM Charset Available
  -----|----------------------|----------------------|----------------------
    0  | xxxxxx11             | $0000-$3FFF (0-16383)     | Yes, at $1000-$1FFF (4096-8191)
    1  | xxxxxx10             | $4000-$7FFF (16384-32767) | No
    2  | xxxxxx01             | $8000-$BFFF (32768-49151) | Yes, at $9000-$9FFF (36864-40959)
    3  | xxxxxx00             | $C000-$FFFF (49152-65535) | No
