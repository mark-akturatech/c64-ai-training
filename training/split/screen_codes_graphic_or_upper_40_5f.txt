# C64 PETSCII and Screen Codes Reference - sta.c64.org - Screen codes $40-$5F (decimal 64-95): in uppercase/graphics mode these display graphic characters; in lowercase/uppercase mode they display uppercase letters A-Z and some symbols. Lists entries $40-$5F including horizontal line, graphic characters and the mapping to letters when in lo/up mode.
