# - Fully Commented Commodore 64 ROM Disassembly (English) - NMI vector entry: disable interrupts (SEI) then JMP ($0318) to the NMI vector handler. No local work done here.

                                *** NMI vector
.,FE43 78       SEI             disable the interrupts
.,FE44 6C 18 03 JMP ($0318)     do NMI vector
