# 6502 Microprocessor - Accumulator addressing: instruction operates directly on accumulator (e.g., LSR with opcode $4A).

  5) Accumulator
  In this mode the instruction operates on data in the accumulator, so no
  operands are needed.
  eg.  LSR - logical bit shift right
       $4A
