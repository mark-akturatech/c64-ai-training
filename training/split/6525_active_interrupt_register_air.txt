# MACHINE - Active Interrupt Register (AIR) layout and clearing behavior. Shows bit layout (A4..A0) and notes that AIR clears on write to AIR. Describes that AIR indicates the currently-active interrupt(s) and is used to inform the CPU which interrupt occurred.

                          Active Interrupt Register
                          -------------------------
            +----+----+----+----+----+  
            | A4 | A3 | A2 | A1 | A0 |  Clears on Write to AIR
            +----+----+----+----+----+
