111           $6F            ARISGN
Result of a Signed Comparison of Accumulator #1 to Accumulator #2

Used to indicate whether the two Floating Point Accumulators have like
or unlike signs.  A 0 indicates like signs, a 255 ($FF) indicates
unlike signs.
